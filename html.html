Why Task Grab is the Perfect Solution for Your Team

Color-Coded Point System (Easy and Clear)

Tasks are color-coded based on difficulty, so your team always knows what they’re getting into:

Green (5 points)

Simple tasks that anyone can handle.

Yellow (10 points)

Medium-difficulty tasks that require a bit more effort.

Red (15 points)

Hard tasks for your most experienced team members.

This simple point system keeps things clear and ensures everyone knows what’s expected, driving accountability and motivation.

Task Tracking Made Easy (Stay on Top of Progress)

Tasks move through three simple stages:

  • Available: Tasks ready to be grabbed.
  • Taken: Once a team member grabs a task, it’s locked in their hands.
  • Complete: Once finished, tasks are grayed out, marking them as done!

This ensures that no task gets lost in the shuffle, and you always know where everything stands.

Leaderboard & Score Tracking (Healthy Competition & Motivation)

See Who’s Leading the Pack: The leaderboard lets your team see who’s earning the most points.

Track Your Progress: Each team member can track their total points, motivating everyone to keep improving.
